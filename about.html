Who Are You?

My name is Garner Evans and I am a 22 year old college graduate living in Oregon. I was born and raised in Texas but moved here following my completion of a bachelors degree in biochemistry. I view life as a pursuit of beauty and strive to use my early twenties as a springboard allowing myself maximum immersion in all the good life has to offer.

A large portion of my time is spent navigating the coldwaters of the pacific northwest via surfboard in pursuit of pristine waves, but when I am not surfing I enjoy metalsmithing, spending time with my brother, skiing reading philiosophical texts, or shaping surfboards. For work, I manage a small coffee shop nestled in a corner of downtown Corvallis, Oregon.

Why Coding?

I have always had a passion for learning new skills and coding seems like one of the most valuable abilities I could learn at this stage in my life. I hope to use it as a path to financial liberation that is becoming increasingly hard to find in our present day society.
